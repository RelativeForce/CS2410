Aston Events is the event organising and tracking tool for the students of Aston University.

Orgainise a guest lecture for members of your favourite society. It also helps Aston build ties with other acedemics. In 2017 alone over 400 guest lectures appeared in seminars and confrences at Aston University. Knowledge should be shared!

Orgainse a culture event to spread awareness of your cause and bring people together. Aston University is one of the most ethnically diverse universities in the United Kingdom and we are proud of it. Together we rise!
